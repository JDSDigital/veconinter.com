Damage Recovery

The welfare of the units is a key factor in the shipping industry; this is why Veconinter provides the Damage Recovery program. A specialized team analyzes EIR’s (Equipment Interchange Receipt) and compares them with Veconinter’s detailed onsite inspections performed by certified International Container Lessors (IICL) personnel in order to transparently determine if any damage that may have been caused to any unit was done at port or by the consignee. Through the strict follow up of each unit, you may rest assured that in case of damage occurred to the equipment we will immediately notify all involved parties in order to activate certified procedures and recover the value of the equipment so the asset can return to be operative.
